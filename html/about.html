about me

I currently live in Cambridge, Massachusetts and am a graduate research assistant in the Active Adaptive Control Lab at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I also work at AIMdyn Inc., where I am developing numerical methods for predicting and controlling nonlinear dynamical systems.

I am passionate about research in controls, machine learning, and robotics. Previously, I was part of the Mezić Research Group at the University of California, Santa Barbara.

In my free time I enjoy playing soccer and drawing.
